Raising Cane's

& The Dream of Franchise Ownership

What is Raising Cane's?

Raising Cane's Chicken Fingers is one of the fastest-growing fast food chains in the United States. Founded in 1996 by Todd Graves in Baton Rouge, Louisiana, the brand built its entire identity around one thing: chicken fingers. No gimmicks, no constantly changing menus — just quality, simplicity, and consistency.

What sets Cane's apart from other fast food chains is its laser focus. While competitors try to do everything, Cane's does one thing and does it better than anyone else. That philosophy is something I deeply admire and hope to carry into my own career in business.

What Makes Cane's Special

  1. The Food
    • Always fresh, never frozen chicken fingers
    • The legendary Cane's Sauce
    • Crinkle-cut fries and Texas toast
    • Ice cold lemonade
  2. The Culture
    • Strong crew culture and team mentality
    • Community involvement and giving back
    • Consistent brand identity across all locations
  3. The Business Model
    • Simple, focused menu reduces waste and errors
    • High volume, fast service
    • Strong brand loyalty among customers

Why I Want to Own a Franchise

My passion for the food industry started early. Growing up around McDonald's, I saw firsthand how a well-run fast food operation works — the systems, the people, the pace. But I always dreamed bigger. Owning a Raising Cane's franchise would let me combine my love for the food industry with my business education at BYU.

Franchise ownership is the perfect intersection of entrepreneurship and proven systems. You get the independence of running your own business while benefiting from a brand that customers already trust and love.

My Goals

  • Graduate from BYU with a degree in Strategy or Global Supply Chain
  • Gain experience in operations and business management
  • Secure funding and apply to become a Raising Cane's franchisee
  • Own and operate multiple locations over time

The Menu

One of the most brilliant things about Raising Cane's is its simplicity. The entire menu fits on one small board:

  • Chicken Finger Combo
  • The Box Combo
  • The Caniac Combo
  • 3 Finger Combo
  • Tailgate (party pack)

Every combo comes with Cane's Sauce, coleslaw, Texas toast, and a drink. That's it. That's the magic.
